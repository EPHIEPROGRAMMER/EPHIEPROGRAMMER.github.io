my projects

All beds have comfortable orthopedic mattresses. We have several communal areas, like a living room with TV, a wooden deck in the back which leads to the garden and a terrace on the roof.

We offer free tea and coffee in the mornings served on the garden terrace and we have a new fully equiped kitchen for our guests to use with all basic spices available. Also we have a small breakfast menu available for people that don't feel like cooking.Our staff speaks Spanish, English and Dutch. All information on tours is available here and we can also book it for you.

We are located right in the center of town, 7 minutes walk from the main bus terminal, 2 blocks east and 2 blocks south

  • Please note:

    • Check out time:6am
    • Cash and credit cards accepted.

Our reception is open from 08:00 till 20:00. But if you arrive early in the morning or late at night, please let us know in advance and we will wait for you.
